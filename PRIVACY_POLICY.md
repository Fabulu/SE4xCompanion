# Privacy Policy

**SE4X Companion (Unofficial)** — last updated 2026-04-05

## TL;DR

This app collects **nothing**. It transmits **nothing**. It has no analytics, no accounts, no ads, and no network requests.

## What the app does

All state (saved games, settings, ship counters, map state, etc.) is stored locally on your device via the standard platform storage APIs (`path_provider` package). Nothing leaves your device.

## What we do NOT do

- No telemetry or analytics
- No user accounts or login
- No advertising or tracking SDKs
- No network access required (no `INTERNET` permission on Android)
- No third-party data sharing
- No cloud sync

## Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. You can also clear app data through your device's app settings to remove everything without uninstalling.

## Platform stores

If you installed the app via Google Play Store, Apple App Store, or similar, those platforms have their own privacy policies that govern downloads, updates, and crash reports the platform may collect. This policy only covers the app itself.

## Children

The app collects no data and does not target children specifically. It is suitable for anyone interested in the board game.

For questions about this policy, open an issue at https://github.com/Fabulu/SE4xCompanion/issues or contact the maintainer listed in [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
