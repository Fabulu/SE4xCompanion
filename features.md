# SE4X Companion App

Digital companion for Space Empires 4X. Replaces both paper sheets.

## Production Sheet
- Full CP ledger with auto-calculated income, maintenance, and remaining CP
- LP/RP/TP tracks for All Good Things Facilities mode
- Tech purchasing with affordability checks (1 level per tech per turn)
- Ship purchasing with tech prerequisite validation
- Colony tracking with growth markers, blockade toggle, mineral/pipeline income
- Facility management (IC/RC/LC/TC) with correct income conversion
- Turn order bid with full-screen secret reveal (pass the phone)
- End Turn auto-computes carry-overs, grows colonies, applies tech, recovers homeworld
- Resource bar always visible across all tabs

## Unpredictable Research
- Full rule 33.0 support: 5 CP per grant, 1d10 per grant, accumulates across turns
- App roll mode with animated tumbling dice and staggered landings
- Manual entry mode for physical dice
- Breakthrough celebration with space particle effects
- Reassign misclicked rolls to a different tech (can't re-roll, just redirect)
- Progress bars per tech showing accumulated vs target

## Ship Technology Sheet
- Individual counter tracking matching the physical sheet (DD#1-6, SC#1-7, etc.)
- Circleable tech levels for Att/Def/Tac/Mov plus type-specific techs (PD, Scanners, Fast, etc.)
- Build button stamps current empire tech (including pending purchases from same turn)
- Upgrade button on outdated counters with auto-costed refits (1 CP per hull size)
- Ship Experience tracking (Green through Legendary)
- Hull-size caps on Attack/Defense enforced automatically

## Alien Economy (Solitaire)
- 1-3 AI opponents with per-turn economy dice rolling
- Full 20-turn schedule with correct roll counts and outcome ranges
- Auto-resolves each d10 to Econ/Fleet/Tech/Defense
- Fleet launch roll tracking
- Fleet log and tech purchase tracking
- Reference table always visible

## Game Management
- Starting fleet wizard (Standard / Standard+Flagship / Custom)
- Multiple saved games with load/switch/duplicate/rename/delete
- Expansion toggles: Close Encounters, Replicators, All Good Things
- Optional rules: Facilities, Logistics, Temporal, Advanced Construction, Ship Experience, Unpredictable Research
- Fleet roster summary with maintenance breakdown

## Supported Content
- Base game
- Close Encounters (all ship types, techs, alien solitaire)
- All Good Things (facilities, LP/RP/TP, supply range, advanced construction, all AGT techs)
- Replicators (Anti-Replicator tech for non-Replicator players)
